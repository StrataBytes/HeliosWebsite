About Helios

Helios is a cutting-edge security robot designed to enhance the safety of educational environments.

Equipped with advanced surveillance technology, Helios is not just a tool for security; it's an interactive companion in the school setting. Built off of the foundation of the Unitree Go2 and modified by us, its friendly design and approachable interface make it ideal for interacting with students and staff, while its robust surveillance capabilities ensure a safe and secure environment.

Helios is a blend of technology, privacy and user-friendliness, setting a new standard in school security solutions and assistance.

Features

Advanced Surveillance Paired With Privacy

Helios combines surveillance capabilities with a strong commitment to privacy. While its sophisticated camera and sensors offer comprehensive coverage of areas which ensures safety in the school, Helios is designed to respect privacy. This balance of vigilant security and ethical privacy standards makes Helios a trusted and innovative presence in the halls.

Approachability

Designed with approachability in mind, Helios features an interactive interface that engages students and staff via small voice commands and a emotion screen. It is planned to support educational and informational requests, making it a valuable addition to the learning environment.

Responsive Design

Helios responds to security alerts promptly and efficiently. Being able to navigate with ease through school, ensuring quick response times to any security concerns that arise.
